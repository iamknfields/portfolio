No overnight non-paying guests, at the time of your reservation you state the number of guests. If any additional guests are staying there is an additional $25 per person that will be charged to your card.

Cancelation Policy

Fourteen (14) days or more prior to date of arrival - deposit is refunded less $30 booking fee.

Less than Fourteen (14) days to date of arrival - deposit will be forfeited.

For Holidays, Special Events, or WEDDING, ANNIVERSARY, HONEYMOON SPECIALS, a thirty (30) day notice is required and a cancellation fee of $100 applies. Less than thirty (30) day - Full deposit will be forfeited.

Any other cancellation notice or No-show will forfeit the entire rental amount. Please READ and by reserving your cabin you agree to the following terms.
