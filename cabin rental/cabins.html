Cabin Rental

Our log cabins are fully furnished with two double beds in the loft and one queen bed downstairs. They are equipped with a full kitchen and bath which are furnished with pots, pans, dishes, microwave, coffee pot, towels, and linens. The cabins also have a screened in back porch, a charcoal grill, and fire pit out front. Otter Cabin and Canoe Rental is the only site in the area with BOTH Satellite TV and WiFi! All cabins have free high speed 1.5 Mbps wireless internet available. Higher speed internet available for purchase.

Otter Creek Cabin

Otter Creek Cabin offers two guests a cozy, fully furnished cabin for a romantic getaway in upper Buffalo River country. The cabin has a main floor and sleeping loft with queen-size bed. We are pleased to offer complimentary WIFI with this cabin rental. The cabin's kitchen features a dining bar and is fully furnished with appliances, basic meal prep utensils and cookware, a coffeemaker and even a dishwasher. In the cabin's bathroom, you'll find a lovely stone walk-in shower, along with towels, soap, and shampoo.
