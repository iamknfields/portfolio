When life gets a little too crazy and chaotic, it's time to get back to the basics and get back to nature. There's no place better to relax and enjoy all that mother nature has to offer than at Otter Cabin & Canoe Rental on the famous Buffalo National River. Plan Your Visit

Camping

Along the banks of the beautiful Buffalo National River there are numerous campgrounds maintained by the National Park Service.

Hiking Trails

Crazy hikes are available for the adrenaline seeking adventurer, as well as gentler hikes for those who wish to take life a little easier.

Mountain Biking

Mountain biking in Arkansas is becoming very popular, with some of the state's finest trails located right here in upper Buffalo River country!

River Trips

No matter who you are, your skill level, or what your schedule is, we have a float trip just for you.

Rock Climbing

The Upper Buffalo National River area boasts some of the best climbing areas east of the Rockies!

Cabin Rentals

Come to our part of the Buffalo River Country and stay in one of our cozy cabins.

Elk Viewing

The elk along the Buffalo River were introduced into the area to replace the now extinct Eastern Elk and have since thrived and roamed wild.

Swimming Holes

Even when the Buffalo National River is too low for floating it still offers a plethora of options for swimming.

Fishing

The Buffalo National River is a clear waterway, which makes it a great choice as a place to fish for bass, perch and catfish.
