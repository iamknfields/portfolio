Canoe Rental

Our outfitter uses Buffaloe Canoes, which are manufactured right here in Newton County, Arkansas.

Rental Information

  • All skill levels are welcome!
  • All rentals include a lifejacket and paddle.
  • Our staff is more than happy to help you carry your canoe as well as help you into it!
  • Reservations are highly recommended for large groups.
  • All renters must fill out a liability waiver.
  • Children under 5 are not allowed to float the river.

Safety & Rules of the River

  • Always keep your life jacket with you.
  • Respect private property.
  • Please Do Not Litter! We want to keep the water and surrounding area as beautiful as possible for everyone.

What to Bring

  • Make sure you dress for the weather. Water shoes are highly recommended.
  • Helpful Items: sun screen, bug spray, & towels. If you forget anything you can grab it at our shop!
  • Coolers & food may be taken on the boats.
  • Fishing poles, binoculars, & waterproof cameras are also welcome.
